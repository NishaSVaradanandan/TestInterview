English Essay Writing - On this page, you will find Essays in English, Latest Essay Topics, examples and format for essay writing. What exactly is essay writing?. Essay are short pieces of writing with a focus on a single subject. It starts with the introduction of the subject, followed by a middle or body in which the author gives his argument as per his understanding of the subject followed by a conclusion paragraph that summarises the findings of the essay.In Colleges and Universities in many Countries (US, UK and many others), Essays have become a major part of Formal Education.
